The Queen's Corgis

Queen Elizabeth II spending time with her corgis in 1973.

Interesting Facts:

  1. Queen Elizabeth II has been fond of corgis since she was a very young child.
  2. She owned more than 30 corgis since she became the Queen of the United Kingdom.
  3. The corgis had been known throughout the world and have been portrayed in many forms of art/entertainment; including the recent film "The Queens Corgi."
